What I Do

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at Illinois Institute of Technology in the Technical Communication program and a predoctoral teaching fellow in the Lewis Department of Humanities. I am expecting to graduate with a Ph.D. in Technical Communication in Spring of 2013.

My Work History

Although I graduated with a degree in English and Rhetoric from the University of Illinois, Urbana, most of my work experience involved a great deal of technical writing. After graduating with my M.A. in English and American Literature from DePaul University, Chicago, I decided that I wanted to enter a field where I was able to continue with my work as a technical communicator. My goal as a student is to bring my specialized knowledge of literature and composition to the field of technical communication.
