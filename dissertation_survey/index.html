Special Survey Information for Twitter Users

Please read before continuing to survey

Thank you for your interest in the survey. Please have your Twitter username ready (you will need it to log in).

Typically, I would ask you to complete the Facebook portion of the survey as well, but I was not able to retrieve posts from your organization's Facebook account. That being the case, you should skip the Facebook portion of the survey.

Why didn't you collect posts from my organization's Facebook account?

There are a few possible reasons:

  • Your organization doesn't have an active Facebook account

  • I did not find your Facebook account when I was collecting data

  • There were no posts from your account during the period in which I was collecting data.

How do I skip the Facebook section?

You will see a link on the login page. When you click on it you will be automatically redirected to the Twitter portion of the survey where you will log in with your username.

What if I can't log in to the Twitter portion?

Please contact me at aroback@iit.edu with the message you received and I will try to correct the problem as soon as I can.
